What is Stoxify

Like smallcase — but for
real-time trade ideas.

smallcase lets advisors sell thematic portfolios. Stoxify lets SEBI Research Analysts deliver live, actionable trade calls the moment they're made.

For Traders

A marketplace of verified trade experts

Browse SEBI-registered Research Analysts, compare their performance history, and subscribe to those that fit your style — intraday, swing, positional, F&O.

The key difference: You get a timestamped BUY / SELL call with exact entry, target and stop-loss — not a static portfolio. Every idea is live, real, and immediately actionable.

A business platform for SEBI-registered RAs

Think of it as your complete advisory business infrastructure — publish ideas, collect subscriptions, stay SEBI compliant, and grow your subscriber base, all in one place.

The key difference: Unlike manual WhatsApp groups or fragmented tools, Stoxify timestamps every idea, handles billing, and gives you a branded professional profile to build trust.
